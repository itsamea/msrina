Profile

Rina Sawayama (澤山 リナ), is a Japanese-British singer-songwriter and model. Born on 16 August 1990 in Niigata, Japan, she moved to London with her family at the age of five. In 2017, she self-released her debut extended play, RINA. After signing to Dirty Hit in 2020, she released her debut studio album, SAWAYAMA, to widespread critical acclaim. She has become a huge voice in both the Asian and LGBT+ communities, and is kind of just really cool.
